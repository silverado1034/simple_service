In Progress
Answer the questions below to determine eligibility.
Question 1
Has the student ever participated in an OSSAA-sanctioned athletic activity at the high school level (grades 9–12)?
Question 2
Are you still living with the same parent/guardian that you lived with last year?
→ Clean – No Waiver. Student has not previously participated; living situation unchanged.
→ Criteria 2 Waiver required. Living situation has changed.
Question 3
Have you participated at a different high school other than Edmonds North HS since starting the 9th grade?
→ Clean – No Waiver. Student has only participated at this school.
Question 4
Have you participated at a different high school other than Edmonds North HS in the last 12 months?
→ Clean – No Waiver. Transfer was more than 12 months ago — student is not subject to current-year waiver requirement.
Question 5
Did the student change schools because your family moved?
Question 6
Did your family move into the boundaries of the new school?
Question 7
Did your family vacate the previous residence?
→ Dual Residence Waiver required. Previous residence was not vacated.
Question 8
Did your family sell or give up any ownership or rights to the previous home (for example, by transferring the deed, ending a lease, or otherwise vacating it)?
→ Dual Residence Waiver required. Ownership or rights to previous home were retained.
Question 9
Will the student live with the same parent(s)/guardian(s) they lived with at their previous school?
→ Bonafide Move. Family completed a full, legitimate relocation.
→ Criteria 2 Waiver required. Student will not live with the same guardian(s).
Question 10
Including this school year, will this be only the second high school where the student has participated in athletics since entering 9th grade?
→ Criteria 2 Waiver required. Student has attended more than two schools.
Question 11
Did you meet the July 15 deadline for submitting a transfer request on the OSDE portal, OR have you re-enrolled in your home district of residence?
→ Criteria 1 Waiver required. Deadline met or re-enrolled in home district.
→ Criteria 2 Waiver required. Deadline was not met and student did not re-enroll in home district.
