OSSAA Change of School Application Guide

Phase 1: Initial Pre-Screening
Is this the first time Sam is participating in an OSSAA-sanctioned activity at the high school level (grades 9-12)?
Is Jenks High School the only high school that Sam has attended since starting the 9th grade?
Phase 2: Change of School Questions
Did Sam change schools because your family moved?
Did your family move into the boundaries of the new school?
Did your family vacate the previous residence?
Did Sam's family sell their former home or give up any ownership or rights to it (for example, by transferring the deed, ending a lease, or otherwise vacating it)?
Criteria-Based Questions
Will Sam be living with the same parent(s)/guardian(s) they lived with at their previous school?
Is this the first time you are changing schools/districts without a corresponding move of residence?
Did you meet the July 15th deadline for submitting a transfer request on the Oklahoma State Department of Education Portal?
